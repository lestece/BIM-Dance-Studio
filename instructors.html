Meet our team of fully qualified instructors!

Sasha Graham

Ballet

After graduating at the Royal Academy of Dance in Ballet Teaching studies, Sasha has performed in a multitude of dance recitals around the world to further her experience.

She’s been teaching Ballet at BIM for 4 years now, and her student ballerinas love her!

Francis Moulin

Jazz

If you ask Francis’ parents how he was as a kid, they’d tell you he’d never stop dancing!

Being obvious what his call was, in 2016 he graduated in Dance and Performance BA (Hons) at UCEN Manchester: 2 months later he was already ideating choreographies in our studio.

Adriana Bassi

Contemporary

Adriana has one of those shiny personalities that are able to conquer a room full of people! And this reflects on her classes: they’re energetic, sensual, soulful.

She graduated in Contemporary Dance BA (Hons) at the Conservatoire for Dance and Drama.

Lizzie McGregor

Afro

Lizzie discovered Afro dance during her path of rediscovering her origins: it was love at first sight.

She gained her Diploma in Dance Teaching at the IDTA and has been with us for 2 years now. Rest assured that you’ll breaka sweat during her classes!
